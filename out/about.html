Our Story

Website Design First — SEO So You Get Found

We started SEO Kings to build websites for trades and local businesses in Bath, Bristol and North East Somerset — sites that look professional, load fast and work on phones. Behind every site is 22 years of development experience and 15 years of graphic design, so you're not funding someone's learning curve.

But a great-looking site is no use if nobody finds it. So we offer SEO and Google Business Profile as add-ons and as standalone services to make sure your site gets found on Google and in the map pack.

Today we work with plumbers, electricians, cleaners, builders and other local businesses across Bath, Bristol, Keynsham, Midsomer Norton, Radstock and the wider area. We've seen what works: a clear website plus a proper Google presence.

Our mission: build websites that work, and provide the SEO and GBP support so they get found.
