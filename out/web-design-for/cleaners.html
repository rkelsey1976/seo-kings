Recommended site structure

Each service type gets its own page — domestic, end of tenancy and deep clean customers search differently and need different content.

  • Home
    DBS and insurance visible, services summary, reviews, quick quote form
  • Domestic / house cleaning
    Highest search volume — regular and one-off cleans
  • End of tenancy cleaning
    High-value, time-sensitive work — specific page ranks cleanly
  • Deep cleaning
    Catches seasonal and one-off search demand
  • Office / commercial cleaning
    Separate page if you do business premises
  • Prices & packages
    Cleaning customers want to know costs upfront — transparency wins trust
  • Service areas
    One page per town you cover — area-specific searches convert well
  • About
    DBS certificate, insurance, how long you've been trading, team photo
