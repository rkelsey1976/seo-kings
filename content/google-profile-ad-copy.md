## SEO checklist (when posting)

- **Keywords in post:** local SEO, Google Business Profile, Bath, BANES, and at least one area (Keynsham, Midsomer Norton, Radstock) or trade (plumber, electrician, builder) where natural.
- **CTA:** Every post has one clear action (free audit, book, get offer) and URL.
- **Button:** Use GBP’s button (e.g. Book now, Learn more, Get offer) and link to homepage or /contact or /google-business-profile as above.
- **Phone:** Include 07702 264 921 in some posts for local relevance and conversions.
- **Rotation:** Rotate ads and copy so the profile stays fresh; reuse top performers.
