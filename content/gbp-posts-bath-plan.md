## How to make Bath GBP rank

**Goal:** Rank for "website design Bath", "plumber Bath", "electrician Bath" and get visibility across Bath and every neighbourhood we serve.

1. **Post at least once a week** — Fresh posts signal an active, relevant business. Rotate these posts so "Bath" and neighbourhood names appear often.

2. **Put Bath + main keyword in the headline** — First ~58 characters matter most. E.g. "Website Design Bath — From £399" or "Get Found on Google in Bath & Every Neighbourhood".

3. **Mention 2–4 neighbourhoods per post** — Odd Down, Widcombe, Walcot, Bathwick, Larkhall, Weston, Bear Flat, Bathampton, Southdown, Batheaston, Bathford. Keeps copy readable and keyword-rich without stuffing.

4. **Use a Bath image** — Local photo (Bath city, Roman Baths, or a neighbourhood) or graphic with "Bath" or "BANES". Helps relevance and engagement.

5. **Link every post to the best Bath page** — /areas/bath, /case-studies/new-decorating, /local-seo or /contact. Same URL in CTA button and (if allowed) in body.

6. **Use the Free Check as an Offer post** — Offer-type posts can get extra visibility. Use the Offer post every 2–3 cycles.

7. **Match your GBP profile** — Business description and services should include "Bath", "website design Bath", "plumber Bath", and neighbourhood names (e.g. Odd Down, Widcombe, Walcot, Bathwick). Same phrases you use in posts.

8. **Reviews and Q&A** — Encourage reviews that mention "Bath" or a neighbourhood. Answer Q&A with Bath-focused, helpful replies.
