## Implementation checklist

When you add each blog to `BlogPost.jsx` and `app/blog/[slug]/page.jsx`:

- [ ] Add entry to `blogPosts` in `src/pages/BlogPost.jsx` (slug, title, description, date, readTime, author, keywords, sections, ctaLinks).
- [ ] Add slug to `BLOG_SLUGS` in `app/blog/[slug]/page.jsx`.
- [ ] Use **areas we serve** in at least one section (Keynsham, Bitton, Saltford, Paulton, Bath, Midsomer Norton, Radstock, Peasedown St John, plus Chew Magna, Temple Cloud, Clutton where relevant).
- [ ] Link to `/areas/keynsham` and `/contact` in CTAs; add `/case-studies/keynsham-electrics`, `/local-seo`, `/google-business-profile`, `/web-design` as relevant.
- [ ] Keep meta description under ~155 characters and title under ~60 for SERPs.
