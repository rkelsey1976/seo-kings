## How to add in Google Business Profile

1. Open [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com) and select your business.
2. Click **Edit profile** (or **Profile** → **Edit profile**).
3. Go to **Services** (or **Services** in the menu).
4. Click **Add service** (or **Add a service**).
5. Enter **Service name** and **Description** from the table above.
6. Save. Repeat for each service you offer.

Use services **1, 2 and 3** as your core list. Add **4** if you sell GBP setup separately, and **5** if you want the free audit as a visible service.
