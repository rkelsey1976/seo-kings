## Slug naming

- Use lowercase and hyphens: `local-seo-bath`, `seo-for-plumbers-bath`.
- Include main keyword (e.g. `local-seo`, `plumbers`, `bath`) where it fits naturally.
- Keep slugs short and readable.

---

## Adding a new post

1. Add the post to the `posts` array in **src/pages/Blog.jsx** (slug, title, excerpt, date, readTime).
2. Add the full post to the `blogPosts` object in **src/pages/BlogPost.jsx** (slug, title, description, date, sections).
3. Add the URL to **public/sitemap.xml** (e.g. `https://seo-kings.co.uk/blog/your-slug`).

Use this list to pick the next posts to build; prioritise “Quick wins” and area/trade combos you want to rank for first.
