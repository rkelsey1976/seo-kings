## 3. THE GAPS (in priority order)

### GAP 1 — No case studies with Bristol businesses
- **What competitors do:** Flex has 10, BMC has 21
- **What you have:** Zero dedicated case study pages
- **Impact:** HIGH — case studies build trust AND rank for "[industry] + [location]" long-tail
- **Effort:** HIGH — need 3-5 real Bristol client case studies, each with screenshots, numbers, quote

### GAP 2 — Industry pages missing
- **What competitors do:** BristolLocalSEO has 9 trade pages (plumbers, electricians, roofers, etc.)
- **What you have:** Zero
- **Impact:** HIGH — "SEO for plumbers Bristol" is a high-intent search that no one is owning well
- **Effort:** MEDIUM — 1 page per trade, ~1500 words each
- **Trade pages to build first:** Plumbers, electricians, builders, roofers, decorators, landscapers (your core market)

### GAP 3 — Location page consolidation
- **What you have:** 20 area pages, BUT split across 4 URL patterns
- **What competitors do:** BMC has 127 location pages, all under one URL pattern

- **Effort:** HIGH — needs 301 redirects + URL consolidation

### GAP 4 — FAQ schema underused

## 5. THE BIGGEST QUICK WIN

**Add FAQ schema to your existing pages.** You already have FAQs on /areas/bristol/ and most service pages. Wrap them in schema.org/FAQPage markup. Cost: 30 mins per page. Impact: rich snippets in SERP, higher CTR, faster indexation.

The second biggest quick win: **write 5 industry pages** (1-2 hours each). Bristol Local SEO has these and ranks well. They're not hard to write — copy the structure of one of theirs, replace with your positioning, add your pricing.

The third biggest quick win: **case studies**. Even 3 of them, written as 1000-1500 word posts with a real Bristol client story, screenshots, before/after metrics, and a quote. This is the single highest-converting content for an agency site.

## 6. WHAT NOT TO DO

  - **Don't try to out-scale BMC.** They have 178 pages, 127 location pages, 220+ reviews, and a full content team. You can't win that game in 2026. Pick a niche (Bristol trades, postcodes around Keynsham, specific service angles) and own it.
  - **Don't write 20 more "areas" pages.** You have 20 areas already. Writing more dilutes your existing ones. Make the existing 20 better instead.
  - **Don't ignore the URL consolidation.** The 4 patterns (/areas/, /seo/X, /local-seo/X, /website-designer-X) are actively hurting you. Pick one and commit.
